üéµ About Sangeetha Music Academy

Sangeetha Music Academy is a dedicated music school that aims to inspire, train, and develop musicians of all ages. Founded with a vision to make music education accessible to everyone, we provide both traditional and modern approaches to learning. For more than a decade, our academy has helped students build strong foundations in Carnatic, Hindustani, and Western music. Whether you are a beginner exploring music for the first time, or an advanced learner preparing for stage performances and exams, we offer structured training for every level.

üåü Our Journey

Since our inception, Sangeetha Music Academy has trained hundreds of students across various musical styles and instruments. With expert teachers, a supportive learning environment, and a strong commitment to excellence, we have earned the trust of parents and students alike. Our approach focuses on guiding learners step by step, helping them build confidence, discipline, and a solid musical foundation.

Over the years, we have expanded our offerings to include beginner-to-advanced level courses, online and offline classes, competition and stage performance training, and specialized coaching for grade examinations such as Trinity and ABRSM.
